35.82.170 - Sign Certificates of Conformance

A. Purpose and intent. This Section establishes procedures and findings for the approval of Sign Certificates of Conformance that are required in compliance with Chapter 35.38 (Sign Standards). The intent of this Section is to ensure that proposed signage is visually attractive and complies with the goals of the County.

B. Applicability. Before erecting, applying, installing, affixing, altering, relocating or projecting as an image any signage, a Sign Certificate of Conformance shall be issued, unless identified as exempt from permit requirements by Chapter 35.38 (Sign Standards).

C. Contents of application. An application for a Sign Certificate of Compliance shall be submitted in compliance with Chapter 35.80 (Permit Application Filing and Processing).
