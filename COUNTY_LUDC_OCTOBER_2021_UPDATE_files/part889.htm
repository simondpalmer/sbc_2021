35.101.020 - Nonconforming Uses of Land and Structures

A nonconforming use may be continued subject to the following provisions, so long as the use remains otherwise lawful.

 

A. Structural change.B. Expansion or extension.d. Submittal of Checklist.e. Enforcement.1. Non-residential uses.b. Damage less than 75 percent.2. Residential uses.4. Applicability of permit requirements.G. Limited exception determinations for certain nonconforming industrial uses.
