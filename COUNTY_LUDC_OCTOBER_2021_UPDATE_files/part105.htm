15. Minor additions, accessory and temporary filming structures.

a. Accessory structures. One story detached accessory structures used as tool or storage sheds, playhouses, gazebos, pergolas, and similar structures, provided that the height does not exceed 12 feet, the floor area (gross) does not exceed 120 square feet, and the structure does not have plumbing or electrical facilities.
