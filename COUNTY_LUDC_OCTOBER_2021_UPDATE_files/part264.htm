1. Determination of applicability.

a. If, upon receipt of an application for grading or the removal of vegetation the Director determines that the site does not contain the pertinent species or habitat, the provisions of this overlay shall not apply.

b. If the provisions of this overlay apply and the Director determines it necessary, a site inspection shall be conducted by a qualified biologist to be selected jointly by the Department and the applicant. Upon completion of the site inspection, and if determined to be necessary, conditions shall be applied to the permit that will protect the riparian corridor to the maximum extent feasible, consistent with the biological habitats goals, objectives, policies, development standards, and actions of the Eastern Goleta Valley Community Plan and the Goleta Community Plan.

2. Relationship to primary zone. Each land use and proposed development within the RC-GOL overlay shall comply with all applicable requirements of the primary zone, in addition to the requirements of this Section.
