35.76.050 - Road Names - Procedure, Standards and Signs

A. Road names required. A road name shall be required for all public and private roads and for any other roads when deemed necessary by the Department; except that a private road located entirely within a contiguous ownership of more than 200 acres shall be exempt from this requirement, unless the property owner files a written request for road naming with the Department.
