A. Changeable copy signs

1. Religious institutions. Each religious institution may be allowed one onsite changeable copy sign using perimeter lighting, not exceeding 24 square feet in area, and not exceeding a height of 10 feet above ground level.

2. Service stations. Service stations may utilize changeable copy only for displaying gas prices.

B. Construction signs. On a building site during construction and to be removed prior to final building inspection: one onsite, temporary, unlighted sign not exceeding eight square feet.
