2. Street access limitations.

a. Core Pedestrian Area. In the Core Pedestrian Area, no vehicular access to new multi-family residential, mixed-use, and commercial development projects shall be permitted via curb cuts and/or driveways on Clark Avenue and Broadway Avenue.

b. Peripheral Pedestrian Area. In the Peripheral Pedestrian Area, vehicular access to new multi- family residential, mixed-use, and commercial development projects via curb cuts and/or driveways on Clark Avenue and Broadway Avenue shall be avoided where feasible.
