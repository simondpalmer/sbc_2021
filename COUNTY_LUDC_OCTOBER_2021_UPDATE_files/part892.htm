d. Submittal of Checklist.

i. Prior to implementation of the temporary expansion or extension, the owner/applicant may, and is encouraged to, submit a completed checklist, revised site plan, photos, and description of proposal describing the temporary expansion or extension and how the requirements of Subsection 35.101.020.B.4.c (Requirements) will be met.

ii. Within 30 days of implementing a temporary expansion or extension, the owner/applicant shall submit a completed checklist, revised site plan, photos, and description of proposal describing the temporary expansion or extension and how the requirements of Subsection 35.101.020.B.4.c (Requirements) have been met.
