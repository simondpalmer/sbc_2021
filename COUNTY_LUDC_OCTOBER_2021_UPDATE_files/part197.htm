c. Side.

(1) A side setback area having a minimum width of 10 feet shall be provided adjacent to a lot zoned residential as shown in Table 1-1 (Zones).

(2) If the project site does not abut a lot zoned residential as shown in Table 1-1 (Zones), then a side setback is not required; however, if provided, it shall not exceed a width of 10 feet.
