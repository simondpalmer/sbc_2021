B. Deadline for filing application.

1. The application for a Demolition and Reclamation Permit shall be filed no later than 180 days after an application to defer abandonment has been denied and administrative appeals have been exhausted in compliance with Chapter 35.102 (Appeals).

2. If an application to defer abandonment has not been filed, an application for a Demolition and Reclamation Permit shall be filed no later than 180 days after an event identified in Section 35.56.030 (Requirement to File an Application) has occurred.

C. Extensions of time. The Director may grant extensions of time for good cause.
