A. Purpose and intent.

1. Purpose. This Section establishes procedures for the recovery of administrative costs, including staff time expended for items such as reports, site inspections, summaries, telephone contacts, correspondence with the owner and any concerned citizens or officials, and related travel time that reasonably relates to the enforcement of the provisions of this Development Code.

2. Intent. The intent of this Section is to recoup administrative costs reasonably related to enforcement.
