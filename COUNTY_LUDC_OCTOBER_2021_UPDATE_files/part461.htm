F. Additional standards for agricultural employee dwellings located in the NTS zone. On a lot zoned NTS:

a. The minimum gross lot area on which an agricultural employee dwelling may be approved is 100 acres.

b. An agricultural employee dwelling shall not be allowed in addition to an accessory dwelling unit, artist studio, or guesthouse.

c. The gross floor area of an agricultural employee dwelling shall not exceed 1,200 square feet.

d. Only one Conditional Use Permit that allows additional dwellings housing five or more employees may be allowed within each project site area covered by an approved Final Development Plan.
