E. Processing.

1. The Zoning Administrator shall hold at least one noticed public hearing on the requested Sign Modification and approve, conditionally approve, or deny the request.

2. Notice of the hearing shall be given and the hearing shall be conducted in compliance with Chapter

35.106 (Noticing and Public Hearings).

3. The action of the Zoning Administrator is final subject to appeal in compliance with Chapter 35.102 (Appeals).
