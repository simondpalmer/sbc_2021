G. Multi-family Residential - Orcutt (MR-O) zone.

1. Parking shall be located in compliance with Section 35.23.130 (Multi-family Residential - Orcutt).

2. Parking areas shall be landscaped in compliance with Section 35.34.100 (Landscaping Requirements for Parking Areas).
