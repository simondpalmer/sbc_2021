D. Processing.

1. Use Determinations under the jurisdiction of the Director. A public hearing shall not be required if the Director is the review authority for the Use Determination.

a. After receipt of an application for a Use Determination, the Department shall review the application in compliance with the requirements of the California Environmental Quality Act.

b. Notice of the application and pending action or action for the Use Determination shall be given in compliance with Section 35.106.050 (Land Use Permits) except that posted notice by the applicant is not required.

c. The action of the Director is final subject to appeal in compliance with Chapter 35.102 (Appeals).
