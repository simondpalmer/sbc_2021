35.89.030 - Conditional Use Permit Requirement

A. A Conditional Use Permit approved in compliance with Section 35.82.060 (Conditional Use Permits and Minor Conditional Use Permits) shall be required in order for a mobilehome park closure to occur.

B. The Commission shall be the review authority for the application for the Conditional Use Permit.
