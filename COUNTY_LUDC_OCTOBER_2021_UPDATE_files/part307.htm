9. Effective date of Subsection C.

a. Gaviota Coast Plan area. The effective date of Subsection C for the Gaviota Coast Plan area is December 9, 2016.

b. Eastern Goleta Valley Community Plan area. The effective date of Subsection C for the Eastern Goleta Valley Community Plan area is November 20, 2015.

c. Mission Canyon Community Plan area. The effective date of Subsection C for the Mission Canyon Community Plan area is May 2, 2014.

d. Santa Ynez Community Plan area. The effective date of Subsection C for the Santa Ynez Community Plan area is November 5, 2009.

e. Summerland Community Plan area. The effective date of Subsection C for the Summerland Community Plan area is June 6, 2014.
