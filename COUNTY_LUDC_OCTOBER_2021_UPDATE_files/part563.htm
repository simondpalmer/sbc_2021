1. In general.

a. The primary purpose of the winery shall be to process wine grapes grown on the winery premises or on other local agricultural lands located within Santa Barbara County and San Luis Obispo County. No more than 50 percent of the grapes processed over a five year period shall be imported from outside of Santa Barbara County and San Luis Obispo County.

b. Retail sales of wine grape products shall be limited to those produced by the winery operator or bottled or grown on the winery premises.
