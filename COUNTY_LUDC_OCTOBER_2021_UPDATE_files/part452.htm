35.42.020 - Accessory Structures and Uses

A. Purpose and applicability. This Section provides standards for accessory structures and uses, where allowed by Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses). Accessory structures, including agricultural accessory structures shall comply with the requirements of this Section, except that mobile home site accessory structures within a Mobile Home Park shall instead comply with the requirements of the MHP District in Section 35.23.080 (MHP Zone Standards).
