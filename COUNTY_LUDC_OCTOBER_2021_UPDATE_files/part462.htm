G. Mobilehomes, manufactured homes, and park trailers.

1. A mobilehome, manufactured home, or park trailer, with or without a permanent foundation, may be used as an agricultural employee dwelling in compliance with the Table 4-1 in Subsection B, above, provided:

a. The mobilehome, manufactured home, or park trailer complies with the California Code of Regulations, Title 25, Division 1, Housing and Community Development.

b. The mobilehome, manufactured home, or park trailer complies with applicable setbacks and building separation requirements required for structures of the zone district in which the mobilehome, manufactured home, or park trailer is located.
