3. Public hearings.

a. Conditional Use Permit and Minor Conditional Use Permits.

(1) The review authority shall consider the Conditional Use Permit or Minor Conditional Use Permit at a noticed public hearing and shall approve, conditionally approve, or deny the request.

(2) Notice of the time and place of the hearing shall be given and the hearing shall be conducted in compliance with Chapter 35.106 (Noticing and Public Hearings).

(3) The action of the review authority is final subject to appeal in compliance with Chapter

35.102 (Appeals).
