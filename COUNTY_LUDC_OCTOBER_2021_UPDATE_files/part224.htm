35.28.030 - Affordable Housing (AH) Overlay Zone

A. Purpose and intent. The Affordable Housing (AH) overlay zone is intended to promote affordable housing production, and implement the policies of the Housing Element by providing substantial incentives to developers through standards of development and performance.
