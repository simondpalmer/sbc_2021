35.42.090 - Community Care Facilities

A. Purpose and applicability. This Section establishes standards for community care facilities where allowed in compliance with Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses). Community care facilities shall be in operated in compliance with State law and in a manner that recognizes the needs of community care operators and minimizes the effects on surrounding properties. Licensing by the appropriate State agency is required for community care facilities unless they are able to operate legally without a license in compliance with State law.
