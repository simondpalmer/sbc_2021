35.101.030 - Nonconforming Structures

A structure that is conforming as to use but nonconforming as to height, lot coverage, setbacks, or other requirements concerning the structure may remain so long as it is otherwise lawful, subject to the following provisions.

 

A. Structural change, expansion, or extension.b. Allowed structural alterations.3. Loss of nonconforming status.2. Structures other than one-family dwellings.b. Damage less than 75 percent.a. The following shall apply to the repair or reconstruction of nonconforming structures.(2) Non-residential agricultural support structures.(3) Non-residential structures, not including agricultural support structures.4. Reconstruction shall commence within 24 months.5. Applicability of permit requirements.
