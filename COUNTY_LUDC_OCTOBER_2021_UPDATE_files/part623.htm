35.55.020 - Applicability

Table 5-1 (Allowed Uses and Permit/Plan Requirements for Oil and Gas Facilities) indicates the plans required for approval of oil and gas facilities. During the review process for the approval of oil and gas facilities, the Commission, or the Board on appeal, shall make the findings or additional findings required in this Chapter. It is the responsibility of the applicant to establish evidence in support of the required findings.
