(2) Landscaping.

(a) AG-I zone. Landscaping plans shall be required in compliance with Section

35.34.050 (Agricultural Zones Landscaping Requirements).

(b) AG-II zone. Landscaping plans shall be required in compliance with Chapter 35.34 (Landscaping Standards).

2. Greenhouses in overlay zones. Greenhouses are limited to 4,000 square feet per lot when located within the Critical Viewshed Corridor Overlay in the Gaviota Coast Plan Area.
