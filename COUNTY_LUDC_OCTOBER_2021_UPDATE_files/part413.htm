C. Gate or entrance sign.

1. Not to exceed two signs at any one entrance.

2. Not to exceed 20 square feet in aggregate sign area at any one entrance.

3. Subject to issuance of a Minor Conditional Use Permit in compliance with Section 35.82.060 (Conditional Use Permits and Minor Conditional Use Permits).
