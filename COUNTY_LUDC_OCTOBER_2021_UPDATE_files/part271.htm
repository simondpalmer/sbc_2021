E. Orcutt Community Plan area.

1. Orcutt Pilot Program permitting procedure. Development that would normally require the approval of a Land Use Permit in compliance with Section 35.82.110 (Land Use Permits) shall instead be approved in compliance with Section 35.82.210 (Zoning Clearance) provided that the development qualifies to be reviewed under the Zoning Clearance process as determined by the Orcutt Pilot Program Checklist (see Appendix E - Orcutt Pilot Project Checklist) incorporated by herein by reference.
