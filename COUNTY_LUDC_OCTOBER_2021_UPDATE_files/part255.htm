A. Purpose and intent.

1. The purpose of the Pedestrian Area - Old Town Orcutt (PA-OTO) overlay zone is to promote pedestrian activity with design standards that emphasize pedestrian safety and comfort, enhance on-


street parking supplies, and foster pleasant development patterns along major street frontages in the downtown portion of Old Town Orcutt.

2. The intent is to maintain the existing mixture of residential and commercial uses, to preserve the character and architectural styles of the neighborhood areas, to encourage development patterns consistent with the historic character of Old Town Orcutt, and to foster a pedestrian oriented environment.
