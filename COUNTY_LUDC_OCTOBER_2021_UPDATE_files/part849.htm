35.86.030 - Notices and Hearings

A. Notice of intention. The Director shall give notice of intention to consider adoption of a Development Agreement in addition to any other notice required by this Development Code for other actions to be considered concurrently with the Development Agreement. The notice shall be given and the hearing shall be conducted in compliance with Chapter 35.106 (Noticing and Public Hearings).
