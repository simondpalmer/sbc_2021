35.101.080 - Nonconforming Due to Lack of a Discretionary Permit

A. Conformity of uses requiring a discretionary permit. A use lawfully existing without the approval of a discretionary permit that would be required by this Development Code, shall be deemed conforming only to the extent that it previously existed (e.g., maintain the same site area boundaries, hours of operation).

B. Previous permits in effect. A use that was authorized by a discretionary permit but is not allowed by this Development Code in its current location may continue, but only in compliance with the discretionary permit.
