4. Neighborhood Commercial (CN) zone.

a. The proposed use is similar in character to those listed as permitted uses in the CN zone.

b. The proposed use is not more injurious to the health, safety or welfare of the neighborhood than those listed as permitted uses in the CN zone because of noise, odor, dust, smoke, vibration, traffic congestion, danger to life, property or other similar causes.
