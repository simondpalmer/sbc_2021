35.23.060 - DR Zone Standards

Proposed development and new land uses within the DR zone shall comply with the following standards, in addition to those in Section 35.23.050 (Residential Zones Development Standards).

A. Maximum lot size and density. The number of dwelling units on a lot shall not exceed the maximum specified by Table 2-11 (DR Zone Maximum Density) for each DR zoning designation shown in Table 2-11 (DR Zone Maximum Density).
