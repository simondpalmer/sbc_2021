c. Residential area requirements.

(1) The residential portion shall include cooking space and sanitary facilities that satisfy the provisions of other applicable codes so that the unit may be legally occupied as a dwelling unit.


(2) The residential portion of the unit shall not occupy more than 50 percent of the gross floor area of the unit. However, in no case shall there be less than 120 square feet of living area exclusive of kitchen, bath, closets and hallways in any live/work unit.

(a) For the purpose of calculating this 120-square foot requirement, the living room, dining room, family room, sleeping area(s), or other area designated for a similar use of a residential nature shall be deemed as living area.

(b) All other habitable rooms except kitchens shall have a minimum floor area of 70 square feet.
