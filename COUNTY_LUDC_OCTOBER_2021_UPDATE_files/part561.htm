35.42.280 - Wineries

A. Purpose and applicability. This Section provides regulations for the development and operation of wineries, where allowed by Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses). The intent is to promote the orderly development of wineries within the County and ensure their compatibility with surrounding land uses in order to protect the public health, safety, natural, and visual resources.
