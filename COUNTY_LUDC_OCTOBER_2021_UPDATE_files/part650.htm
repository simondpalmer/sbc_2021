35.56.160 - Post Approval Procedures

A. Post approval procedures regarding changes to an approved Demolition and Reclamation Permit. The procedures and requirements in Subsection C (Substantial Conformity Determination), Subsection D (Amendments) and Subsection E (Revisions) of Section 35.84.040 (Changes to an Approved Project) shall apply following the decision on an application for a Demolition and Reclamation Permit.
