35.28.140 - Hazardous Waste Management Facility (HWMF) Overlay Zone

A. Purpose and intent. The Hazardous Waste Management Facility (HWMF) overlay zone provides a mechanism for the siting of off-site hazardous waste management facilities and is intended to ensure that the facilities are sited consistent with both the requirements of the Hazardous Waste Element of the Comprehensive Plan and the primary zone.
