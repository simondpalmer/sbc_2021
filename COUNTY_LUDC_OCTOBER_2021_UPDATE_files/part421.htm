35.38.100 - Signs Allowed in Shopping Centers

A. Conformance with Overall Sign Plan. All signs located within a shopping center, except service station signs, shall conform to an Overall Sign Plan approved in compliance with Section 35.82.150 (Overall Sign Plan). Signs for service stations may be allowed compliance with Section 35.38.090 (Signs Allowed in Commercial and Industrial Zones Outside of Shopping Centers).
