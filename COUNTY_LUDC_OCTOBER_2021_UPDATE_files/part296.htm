35.30.040 - Reserved

35.30.050 - Density

A. The densities specified in the Comprehensive Plan are maximums and may be reduced through discretionary project review if the review authority determines that a reduction is warranted by conditions specifically applicable to a site, including topography, geologic or flood hazards, habitat areas, or steep slopes.

B. Density may be increased for an affordable housing project in compliance with Housing Element policies.
