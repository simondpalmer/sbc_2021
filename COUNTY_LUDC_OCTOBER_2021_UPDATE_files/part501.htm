B. Conference centers.

1. Prohibited uses. A conference center shall not be used for sales promotional groups or for groups assembled primarily for social purposes.

2. Time limits. Conference centers may be used by persons assembled for periods of time not to exceed

60 days. The 60-day limitation may be extended in special circumstances by the Board on recommendation by the Commission.
