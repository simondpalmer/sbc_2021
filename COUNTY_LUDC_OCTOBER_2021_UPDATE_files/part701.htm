35.76.030 - Areawide Address Numbering System

A. Boundaries. The boundaries of each areawide system shall be established by Board resolution after a recommendation by the Commission. The adopted boundaries shall be identified on the Countywide Official Address Numbering Plan Base Map prepared by the County of Santa Barbara Fire Department, hereinafter referred to as the Fire Department.
