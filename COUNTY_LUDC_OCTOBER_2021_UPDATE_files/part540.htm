35.42.240 - Rural Recreation

A. Purpose and applicability. This Section provides standards for rural recreation, where allowed in compliance with Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses).


B. Allowable uses and permit requirement. Low-intensity recreational development (e.g., recreational camps, hostels, campgrounds, retreats, and guest ranches, trout farm, rifle range, and duck shooting farm) may be allowed subject to a Conditional Use Permit in compliance with Section 35.82.060 (Conditional Use Permits and Minor Conditional Use Permits) provided the development complies with the applicable standards included in Subsection C. (Standards) below.

 

C. Standards.a. Retreats.1. Campgrounds.2. Farmstay.3. Fishing operation.4. Horseback riding.
