C. Permit and processing requirements.

1. Development Plan approval required. A Final Development Plan in compliance with Section

35.82.080 (Development Plans) and a Land Use Permit in compliance with Section 35.82.110 (Land Use Permits) is required for one or more structures that total 10,000 or more square feet in gross floor area, or where onsite structures and outdoor areas designated for sales or storage total 20,000 square feet or more.
