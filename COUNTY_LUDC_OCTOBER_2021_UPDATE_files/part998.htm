K. Definitions, "K."

Kennel, Animal Boarding. A facility for the grooming, keeping, boarding or maintaining of four or more dogs, six months of age or older. See also "Veterinary Clinic, Animal Hospital.‚Äù

1. Kennel, Commercial. A kennel as defined above which may include boarding facilities, where the dogs are for other than private enjoyment of the occupants of the premises, and where boarding services are offered to the public.

2. Kennel, Private. A kennel as defined above where the dogs are kept for the private enjoyment of the occupants of the premises.


Kill Floor. An area within an agricultural building or structure where livestock raised on the premises are slaughtered, packed, or wrapped on a commercial basis, but not including processing beyond the raw state.

Kitchen. A room, all or any part of which is designed, built, equipped, maintained, used, or intended to be used for the preparation and cooking of food.
