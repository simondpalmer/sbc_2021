A. Qualification as an applicant.

1. Only a person who has legal or equitable interest in the real property that is the subject of a proposed Development Agreement, or their authorized agent may apply to the County for the approval of a Development Agreement.

2. The Director may require an applicant to submit proof of their interest in the real property and of the authority of an agent to act for the applicant.

3. Before processing the application, the Director shall obtain the opinion of the County Counsel as to the sufficiency of the applicantâ€™s interest in the real property to enter into the Agreement.
