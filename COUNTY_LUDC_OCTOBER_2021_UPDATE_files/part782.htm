1. Findings for all Modifications.

a. The project is consistent with the Comprehensive Plan, including any applicable community or area plan.

b. The project complies with the intent and purpose of the applicable zone including overlays, and this Development Code.

c. The Modification is minor in nature and will result in a better architectural or site design, as approved by the Board of Architectural Review, and/or will result in greater resource protection than the project without the Modification.

d. The project will be compatible with the neighborhood, and will not create an adverse impact to aesthetics, community character, or public views.

e. Any Modification of parking or loading zone requirements will not adversely affect the demand for on-street parking in the immediate area.

f. The project will not be detrimental to existing ambient noise levels, physical access, light, solar exposure, or ventilation on or off the subject site.

g. Any adverse environmental impacts will be mitigated to a level of insignificance.
