35.102.030 - Appeals to the Zoning Administrator

A. Decisions appealed to the Zoning Administrator. The following decisions of the Director may be appealed to the Zoning Administrator:

1. Any decision by the Director to approve, conditionally approve, or deny an application for a Land Use Permit for a temporary use in compliance with Section 35.42.260 (Temporary Uses and Trailers) may be appealed to the Zoning Administrator.

B. Hearing Required. The Zoning Administrator shall hold a hearing on the appeal no later than 12 hours prior to the time the event is scheduled to commence.

C. Notice required. Notice of the date, time and location of the hearing shall be provided to the applicant, the appellant, if different than the applicant and any interested person who has filed a written request for notice with the Department.

D. Action on appeal. The Zoning Administrator shall affirm, reverse, or modify the decision of the Director as soon as practicable following the filing of the appeal and in no case later than the time the temporary use is scheduled to commence. The action of the Zoning Administrator is final and not subject to appeal.
