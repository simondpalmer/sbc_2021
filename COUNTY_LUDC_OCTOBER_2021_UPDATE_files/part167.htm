A. CN zone standards.

1. Enclosure of activities required. All retail uses that supply commodities and service uses allowed by Table 2-12 (Allowed Land Uses and Permit Requirements for Commercial Zones) shall occur within a completely enclosed building, except for service stations and other uses that commonly include an outdoor component provided any use that occurs outside of a completely enclosed building is appropriately screened and such screening is specifically approved by the review authority.

2. Storage and trash enclosures. Areas for trash or outdoor storage shall be enclosed and screened to conceal all trash or stored material from public view.
