2. Building coverage.

a. Residential uses only. Lot coverage for buildings and structures containing only residential uses shall be as required by Section 35.26.040 (Special Purpose Zones Development Standards) for the OT-Residential zone.

b. Core Pedestrian Area. In the Core Pedestrian Area there shall be no restriction on building coverage for buildings and structures containing uses other than exclusive residential uses.

c. Peripheral Pedestrian Area. In the Peripheral Pedestrian Area not more than 65 percent of the gross area shall be occupied by buildings and structures containing uses other than exclusive residential uses.

3. Parking. Off street parking shall be provided as required by Section 35.36.120 (Standards for Mixed Use Zones and Uses).
