2. Additional finding required for Final Development Plans.

a. Substantial conformity. The plan is in substantial conformity with any previously approved Preliminary Development Plan, except when the review authority considers a Final Development Plan for which there is no previously approved Preliminary Development Plan. In this case, the review authority may consider the Final Development Plan as both a Preliminary and Final Development Plan.

(1) If the Final Development Plan is under the jurisdiction of the Director, and the Director cannot find that the Final Development plan is in substantial conformity with the previously approved Preliminary Development Plan, the Director shall refer the Final Development Plan to the review authority that approved the Preliminary Development Plan for a decision on the Final Development Plan.
