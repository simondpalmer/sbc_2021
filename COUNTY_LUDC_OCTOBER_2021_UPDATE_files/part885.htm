35.100.070 - Boards of Architectural Review

A. Appointment. The Boards of Architectural Review shall be constituted in compliance with Article V of Chapter 2 of the County Code.

B. Duties and authority. The Boards of Architectural Review shall review all applicable project proposals in compliance with Section 35.82.070 (Design Review) and shall report their findings to the applicable review authority specified in Table 8-1 (Review Authority).
