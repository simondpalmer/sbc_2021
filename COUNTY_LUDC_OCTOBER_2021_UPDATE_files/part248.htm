C. Permit and processing requirements.

1. Referral and determination. Prior to the approval of a Land Use Permit in compliance with Section

35.82.110 (Land Use Permits) or a Zoning Clearance in compliance with Section 35.82.210 (Zoning Clearances) for all development subject to the FA overlay zone;

a. The applicant shall be referred to the Flood Control Agency for a determination as to whether the development is subject to the requirements of County Code Chapter 15A. If the Flood Control Agency determines that the proposed development is subject to Chapter 15A, then the development shall comply with the requirements of Chapter 15A.


b. The applicant shall obtain the appropriate clearance or receive a written exemption from the Flood Control Agency.
