35.57.030 - Allowed Uses and Permit Requirements for Wind Energy Conversion Systems

A. Allowed Uses and Permit Requirements. Table 5-2 (Allowed Uses and Permit Requirements for Wind Energy Conversion Systems) identifies the types of wind energy conversion systems that are allowed by this Development Code in each zone, and the planning permit required to establish each use, in compliance with Section 35.20.030 (Allowable Development and Planning Permit Requirements). A wind energy conversion system that is not listed in the table is not allowed.

B. Where the last column (Specific Use Regulations) in Table 5-1 includes a section number, the referenced Section may establish other requirements and standards applicable to the wind energy conversion system.
