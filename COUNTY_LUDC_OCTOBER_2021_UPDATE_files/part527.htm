35.42.190 - Home Occupations

A. Purpose and applicability. This Section provides development and operational standards for home occupations where allowed by Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses). The intent is to prevent any adverse effects on the residential enjoyment of surrounding residential properties.

B. Applicability. The provisions of this Section shall apply to all home occupations which include Cottage Food Operations and In-home Retail Sales. Home occupations may be permitted in any dwelling in any zone including nonconforming dwellings.
