35.34.030 - Landscape Plans

A. Landscape plans. Landscape plans shall be required in compliance with Section 35.34.050 through Section

35.34.100 below, as a condition of an approved planning permit, and where a Conditional Use Permit in compliance with Section 35.82.060 (Conditional Use Permits and Minor Conditional Use Permits) or a Development Plan in compliance with Section 35.82.080 (Development Plans) is required. A landscape design professional shall prepare landscape plans. Landscape plans shall be in compliance with the Department handout, “Landscape Plan and Performance Security Procedures.”
