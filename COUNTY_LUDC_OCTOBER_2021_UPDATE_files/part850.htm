B. Public hearings.

1. Commission and Board hearings required. The Commission shall conduct at least one public hearing on a proposed Development Agreement before making a recommendation to the Board on the Agreement, and the Board shall conduct at least one hearing before making a decision on the application.

2. Hearing notice. Notice of the hearing shall be given and the hearing shall be conducted in compliance with Chapter 35.106 (Noticing and Public Hearings). Failure of any person entitled to notice required by law or this Development Code does not affect the authority of the County to enter into a Development Agreement.
