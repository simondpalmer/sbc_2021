A. Structural change, expansion, or extension.

1. Enlargements or extensions allowed in limited circumstances.

a. Except as listed in Subsection A.1.b (Allowed structural alterations), below or otherwise provided in this Development Code, a nonconforming structure shall not be enlarged, extended, moved, or structurally altered unless the enlargement, extension, etc., complies with the height, lot coverage, setback, and other requirements of this Development Code.
