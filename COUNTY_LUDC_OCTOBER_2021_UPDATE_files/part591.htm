35.50.020 - Applicability

The various chapters in Article 35.5 (Oil and Gas, Wind Energy, and Cogeneration Facilities) identify the types of oil and gas facilities, wind energy systems and cogeneration facilities that are allowed in the County within certain zones established by Section 35.14.020 (Zoning Map and Zones); determine required types of planning permits and plans; and provide regulations for the operation of the oil and gas facilities, wind energy systems and cogeneration facilities.
