35.64.010 - Program Purpose and Intent, Description and Goals

A. Purpose and intent. The provisions of this Chapter implement the Transfer of Development Rights program. The intent of this program is to transfer development potential from eligible Naples lots to eligible receiving sites along the South Coast of Santa Barbara County in furtherance of Coastal Land Use Plan Policy 2-13. The overriding purpose is to extinguish the rights to develop Naples lots determined to have the greatest public benefit by the Board.
