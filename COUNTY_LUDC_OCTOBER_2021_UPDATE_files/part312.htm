4. Interior and odd-shaped lots.

a. Interior lot. The setback requirements of the applicable zone shall not apply, and a structure on an interior lot shall have a setback of at least 10 feet from all property lines (25 feet in the EX-1 zone), and the total area in square feet of all setbacks shall equal the total area in square feet of all setbacks otherwise required by the applicable zone for a non-interior lot. See Figure 3-7 (Interior Lot).

b. Odd-shaped lots. In the case of odd-shaped lots, the Director shall determine the required setbacks, which widths and depths shall approximate as closely as possible the required widths and depths of corresponding setbacks on rectangular lots in the applicable zone district.
