H. Planned Residential Development (PRD) zone.

1. Construction and design. Parking areas shall be arranged to prevent through traffic to other parking areas.

2. Landscape/screening of parking areas. Uncovered parking areas shall be screened and landscaped in compliance with Subsection 35.34.060.D (Planned Residential Development (PRD) zone) and Section 35.34.100 (Landscaping Requirements for Parking Areas).

3. Agricultural product sales. A minimum of two parking spaces shall be provided. Parking spaces shall be located no closer than 20 feet to the right of way of any street.
