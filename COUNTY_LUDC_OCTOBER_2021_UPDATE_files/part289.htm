35.30.010 - Purpose

This Article expands upon the standards of Article 35.2 (Zones and Allowable Land Uses) by addressing the details of site planning and project design. These standards are intended to ensure that all development:

A. Produces an environment of stable and desirable character;

B. Is compatible with existing and future development; and

C. Protects the use and enjoyment of neighboring properties, consistent with the Comprehensive Plan.
