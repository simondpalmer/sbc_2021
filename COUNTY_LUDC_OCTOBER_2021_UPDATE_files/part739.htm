2. Additional findings required for sites zoned MT-GOL (Mountainous Goleta) zone.

a. The proposed project will not cause significant erosion, sedimentation, runoff, siltation, or an identified significant adverse impact to downstream water courses or water bodies.

b. The proposed project will not cause any significant adverse effect on environmentally sensitive habitat areas.
