35.30.180 - Storm Water Runoff Requirements

A. Applicability. The following development or redevelopment is subject to the requirement that project- appropriate controls are in place to prevent or minimize water quality impacts:

1. Residential subdivisions with 10 or more dwelling units.

2. Commercial development of 0.5 acres or greater.

3. Parking lots of 5,000 square feet or more or have 25 or more parking spaces and are potentially exposed to storm water runoff.

4. Automobile repair shops.

5. Retail gasoline outlets.

6. Restaurants.

7. One-family residences located on slopes of 20 percent or greater.

8. Any new development or redevelopment exceeding one acre.

B. Processing. No permit for any development listed in Subsection A (Applicability) above, shall be approved except in compliance with the Comprehensive Plan, and the California Environmental Quality Act if applicable.
