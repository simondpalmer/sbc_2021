35.38.090 - Signs Allowed in Commercial and Industrial Zones Outside of Shopping Centers

A. Identification and gate or entrance signs. Identification signs and gate or entrance signs in compliance with Subsection 35.38.080.B (Identification sign) and Subsection 35.38.080.C (Gate or entrance sign) above.

 

B. For sale, lease, or rent signs.C. Wall signs.D. Under canopy signs.E. Projecting signs.F. Arcade signs.H. Freestanding signs.
