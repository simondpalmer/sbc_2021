B. Applicability.

1. Final Development Permit required. No permit shall be issued for any development, including grading, for any property subject to this Section until a Final Development Plan has been approved in compliance with this Section.

2. All portions of site to be included. No portion of a property not included within the boundaries of the Development Plan shall be entitled to any development permits.
